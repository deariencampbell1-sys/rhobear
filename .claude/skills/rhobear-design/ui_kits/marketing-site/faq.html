Frequently asked,
honestly answered.

Six things people ask before they boot the hub for the first time. The answers don't dress anything up.

Do you see my data?
No. RHOBEAR runs on your machine or your VPS. We are not the middleman between you and the models you choose.
Is this for devs or normal people?
Both. The hub is friendly enough for a first-timer. The CLI + workbench stack is direct enough for someone who already knows what MCPs and review gates are.
Do I need to be technical?
The founder built RHOBEAR with childhood web-design background plus adult team-leadership skills. No CS degree. No tutorial habit. He learned to communicate with the fleet, not write the syntax by hand. If you can sequence a project into steps, the fleet does the rest.
What if I don't have API keys yet?
Use the bundled local model first. Plug in paid providers later when you want more horsepower.
Can I run it on a cheap VPS instead of my laptop?
Yes. The product claim is not "our cloud." It's "your machine, your VPS, your call."
Are payments and the public installer already live?
No. The landing page points to the repo install path because that's the honest thing to do while launch plumbing is being closed out.
