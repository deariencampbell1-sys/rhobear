OpenClaw — the local bridge we route through.

OpenClaw is an open-source local routing layer that lets RHOBEAR reach your tools, agents, and machine safely. We didn't invent it. We integrated it deeply into the substrate so the rest of the recipe has somewhere clean to talk to your local environment.

OPENCLAW · LOCAL BRIDGE
HOOK

OpenClaw is the local bridge that lets RHOBEAR reach your tools, agents, and machine safely.

TECH

A local routing layer for agent/tool communication, runtime adapters, workspace state, and self-hosted coordination APIs.

WE INTEGRATE — WE DIDN'T BUILD IT

Honest receipt.

OpenClaw is the work of the Earendil-works lineage — same family as Pi. RHOBEAR ships it as a Tauri sidecar, but the project is theirs. If you swap OpenClaw out for another local bridge, the substrate doesn't break — the contract is the seam, not the implementation.
