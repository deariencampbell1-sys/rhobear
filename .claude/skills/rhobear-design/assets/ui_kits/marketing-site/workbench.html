WHY THIS PAGE MATTERS MOST

Most agent products give you a chat. We gave you a workbench.

A chat is one model, one channel, no memory. The workbench is a team of named workers running in parallel lanes, each one with its own session, its own diff, its own gate. You see all of them at once. You promote what's right. You reject what isn't. You stay in charge.
